## Findings by Severity

### CRITICAL (1)

1. **Contact form non-functional** — `siteConfig.js` has `formspreeId: 'YOUR_FORMSPREE_ID'`
   - *Impact:* Users cannot contact the artist
   - *Fix:* Replace with actual Formspree form ID or move to .env variable
   - *Effort:* 2 min

### HIGH (2)

2. **FloatingGalleryHero exceeds 300-line limit** — 717 lines of tightly-coupled animation logic
   - *Impact:* Hard to maintain, debug, or test
   - *Fix:* Extract animation logic into custom hooks, split rendering
   - *Effort:* 30 min

3. **Hard-coded pixel values in FilmCard** — 648px, 476px, 652px offsets
   - *Impact:* Layout breaks on ultra-wide or non-standard displays
   - *Fix:* Use relative units or CSS calc()
   - *Effort:* 15 min

### MEDIUM (5)

4. **Missing 404 route** — Unknown URLs show blank page
   - *Impact:* Poor UX for mistyped URLs or broken links
   - *Fix:* Add catch-all route with NotFound component
   - *Effort:* 5 min

5. **Hardcoded scroll threshold in Films.jsx** — `HERO_SCROLL_THRESHOLD = 1000`
   - *Impact:* Inconsistent behavior on different viewport heights
   - *Fix:* Use `window.innerHeight` multiplier
   - *Effort:* 2 min

6. **Body overflow conflicts** — Both SmoothScrollContext and Lightbox set `document.body.style.overflow = 'hidden'`
   - *Impact:* Opening/closing lightbox may break smooth scroll state
   - *Fix:* Coordinate overflow management through a shared mechanism
   - *Effort:* 10 min

7. **No spam protection on contact form** — No honeypot field
   - *Impact:* Form susceptible to bot spam
   - *Fix:* Add honeypot field (Formspree supports `_gotcha`)
   - *Effort:* 3 min

8. **Audio-video sync drift** — 2-second throttle in HeroSection
   - *Impact:* Audio may drift noticeably from video on slow connections
   - *Fix:* Reduce throttle interval or use requestVideoFrameCallback
   - *Effort:* 5 min

### LOW (5)

9. **No lazy loading on film thumbnails** — All images load at once
10. **No image preloading in Lightbox** — Next/prev images load on demand
11. **No touch gestures in Lightbox** — Mobile users can't swipe
12. **Counter-intuitive animationSmoothing API** — Inverted parameter logic
13. **No passive wheel listeners** — May block scrolling on some browsers
